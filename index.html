We build and operate it; you rent it — that's why there's no $3,000 bill. Your domain can be registered in your name, and if you ever cancel, you keep your domain and phone number. No hostage situations.

You built my site without asking. Is that weird?

A little! But it's just your public Google information — your business name, star rating, and phone number — arranged into a website. Nothing private, nothing you haven't already published. If you want it gone, one reply deletes it permanently.

What if I want changes?

Reply with what you want changed — new photos, prices, services, anything. Done same week, included in the $99. That's the "we run it for you" part.

None — on purpose. No WordPress, no Wix, no page builders or plugins. Custom code, which is why the sites load instantly on a phone, have nothing to hack or constantly update, and never lock you in: standard web code runs anywhere, so if you ever leave, it leaves with you.
